Jackson's Comic Book Recommendations

Four of my favorite stories

Invincible(Robert Kirkman)

For my first recommendation, it will have to be Invincible. This is one of my most favorite comics of all time, it is about a boy named Mark with superpowers and his struggles with growing up, becoming a superhero, and coming to terms with his alien side of himself. This story is incredibly dark and has some of the best villains and characters in any story ever told in my opinion. It also has the first 13 issues animated on amazon prime video.
